Pro Tips to Maximize Progress

  • ✅ Consistency beats intensity—stick to your plan, even on tough days.
  • 🥗 Don’t skip meals—fuel equals energy and recovery.
  • 💧 Stay hydrated—aim for at least 35ml per kg of body weight daily.
  • 📊 Track your stats weekly—measure progress, not perfection.
  • 🧠 Recovery matters—quality sleep and rest days are essential to gains.
