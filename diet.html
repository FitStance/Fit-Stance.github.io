Diet Plans

Nutrition is the foundation of progress. What you eat shapes your energy, performance, mood, and recovery. This page gives you a clear, practical path—sample calorie plans to follow, an Indian-food chart to understand calories and macros per 100 g/ml, and an easy recipe hub to make eating healthy simple and sustainable.

For best results, personalize first. Head to the Tools page and find your BMI, BMR, and TDEE to understand how much energy your body needs every day and how your macros should be split for your goal. Once you know your daily target, pick a suitable plan below and use the food chart to build flexible meals that fit your lifestyle.
